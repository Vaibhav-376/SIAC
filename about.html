SIAC History

SIAC was established as a Trust under the Indian Trust Act, 1882, on 15th September 2008, with its headquarters in Delhi. That same year, it launched five IT education centers aimed at providing opportunities for economically disadvantaged youth in Delhi and Uttar Pradesh. Through generous contributions from trustees and well-wishers, SIAC successfully trained approximately 200 beneficiaries within the first six months of operation.

  • SIAC provides meaningful work and training opportunities to economically disadvantaged youth, helping them gain valuable skills for future success.
  • SIAC faces challenges in providing resources and support, but these challenges are met with the goal of creating better opportunities and satisfaction for the youth they serve.
  • Despite obstacles, SIAC remains dedicated to its mission, ensuring that the youth benefit from the training programs and ultimately overcome socio-economic barriers.
